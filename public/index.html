Biography

Tommy Kuan-Wei Ho is currently a graduate student majoring Computer Science in University of Washington pursuing his Master in Technology Innovation degree.

Tommy had research experiences on natural language processing, computer vision and robotics related topics and implemented several machine learning projects back in NTHU. He also had industrial experiences dealing with practical machine learning for business productionin two distinguished startups and one international corporation.
