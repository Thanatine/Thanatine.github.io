Kuan-Wei Ho

Biography

Kuan-Wei Tommy Ho is a graduate B.Sc. in Computer Science from National Tsing Hua University. His research interests include artificial intelligence, data science, machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, robotics and their application.

Tommy had research experiences back in NTHU conducting rigorous research on natural language processing, computer vision and robotics related topics. He also had several industrial experiences dealing with machine learning practicability for production and exploring the possibility of machine learning when facing real world challenges in two distinguished startups and international corporation.
